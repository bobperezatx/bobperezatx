## What I Design

![GTM Intelligence Systems](https://img.shields.io/badge/GTM_Intelligence_Systems-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Automated multi-cadence research pipelines that continuously gather market data, competitive signals, and account insights, feeding raw sources into unified intelligence layers that power sales and marketing decisions.

![ABM & Account Targeting](https://img.shields.io/badge/ABM_&_Account_Targeting-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
AI-powered account clustering, lookalike audience generation, and evidence-based scoring. Goes beyond firmographics to incorporate technographic shifts, buying signals, and intent patterns, feeding prioritized targets into Apollo, Clay, and ZoomInfo.

![Competitive Intelligence](https://img.shields.io/badge/Competitive_Intelligence-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Evidence taxonomy systems with confidence scoring, distinguishing between what's observed, what's claimed, and what's inferred. Structured output for executive dashboards and real-time sales enablement.

![Demand Gen Automation](https://img.shields.io/badge/Demand_Gen_Automation-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Campaign simulation engines that model budget allocation, channel tradeoffs, and regional scenarios before committing spend. Product update pipelines that convert feature announcements into account-aware outreach. Email analytics systems that generate executive performance narratives from raw campaign data.

![Knowledge & Learning Systems](https://img.shields.io/badge/Knowledge_&_Learning_Systems-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Personal knowledge capture pipelines that automatically enrich and structure saved content (articles, videos, threads, repos) into a queryable knowledge base. Every source is hand-vetted, so queries search high-quality curated material, not the open web. Answers come from trusted sources, not cold search results.

Additional production use cases built across **Outreach & Messaging**, **Content Operations**, and **Process & Ops Infrastructure**.
