## What I Design

![Account Intelligence](https://img.shields.io/badge/Account_Intelligence-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
ICP scoring, account enrichment, cluster discovery, and lookalike modeling. Turns minimal inputs into full intelligence profiles on target accounts: firmographics, technographics, buying signals, and competitive displacement analysis, feeding prioritized targets into Apollo, Clay, and ZoomInfo.

![Campaign Intelligence](https://img.shields.io/badge/Campaign_Intelligence-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
ABM account research, outreach personalization, competitor attack assessments, and campaign strategy. Designs and executes account-specific campaign motions with research-backed messaging that references specific signals rather than generic value propositions.

![Buyer Intelligence](https://img.shields.io/badge/Buyer_Intelligence-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Sentiment analysis, personalized product updates, and onboarding intelligence. Tracks buyer signals and maps them to relevant engagement moments, identifying where prospects are in their journey and what angle is most likely to land.

![Decision Intelligence](https://img.shields.io/badge/Decision_Intelligence-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Demand gen simulation engines, competitive intelligence orchestration, workflow evaluation frameworks, and cross-reference tools. Systems that present options with explicit tradeoffs and confidence intervals rather than prescribing one answer, enabling leadership to choose strategically.

![Content Intelligence](https://img.shields.io/badge/Content_Intelligence-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Content audits that assess coverage gaps, competitive alignment, and asset reuse opportunities across existing libraries. Surfaces what's missing, what's redundant, and where to focus production investment.

![Content Production](https://img.shields.io/badge/Content_Production-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
AI-assisted whitepapers, newsletters, and product news generation. Maintains brand voice and reduces production time while keeping output grounded in verified research rather than generic templates.

![GTM Operations](https://img.shields.io/badge/GTM_Operations-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
SOP generators, meeting insight systems, and LinkedIn engagement outreach automation. The infrastructure layer that makes GTM workflows faster, more consistent, and auditable: state management, deduplication, and process automation built for production use.

![Productivity](https://img.shields.io/badge/Productivity-1e40af?style=for-the-badge)
Personal knowledge capture pipelines and a multi-cadence GTM intelligence platform that compounds over time. Every source hand-vetted so queries return trusted, curated results, not cold search hits.
